What is expository?

Expository is an initiative to establish career tracks for BITSians to help them make informed choices regarding future opportunities.

What does expository include?

This repository of experiences contains carefully curated courses on topics crucial for placements, podcasts that provide guidance in multiple domains from BITSians on the other side and data insights that will provide a realistic perspective to all your concerns.
